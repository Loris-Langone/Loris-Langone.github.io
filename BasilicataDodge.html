Welcome to the BASILICATA DODGE Project

Overview:
"Basilicata Dodge" is a 2D game made with Unity. The player controls a wild boar and must dodge rocks falling from above. The game has colorful graphics and a background soundtrack that creates an engaging atmosphere. The player's score increases with every successfully dodged rock, and the game becomes harder as the rocks fall faster.

Gameplay:
🔹 The player controls the wild boar using the mouse or the keyboard arrow keys.
🔹 The wild boar can move left and right to avoid the rocks falling from the top.
🔹 The rocks fall at different speeds and in random positions on the screen.
🔹 The player gets points every time they successfully dodge a rock.
🔹 The game ends when a rock hits the wild boar. Then, the final score is displayed.

Game Goals:
🔹 Dodge as many rocks as possible to get the highest score.
🔹 Challenge yourself and your friends to see who can get the highest score.

Controls:
🔹 Use the mouse to move the wild boar left or right.
🔹 Use the keyboard arrow keys (left and right) to move the wild boar.

Artwork:
🔹 Playable Character: A pixel art wild boar. It is brown with black fur and visible tusks. Its eyes are a bright light-blue color, showing intensity and determination.
🔹 Rocks: Heavy rocks falling from above.
🔹 Backgrounds: The backgrounds are designed in pixel art to create a lively and changing atmosphere. Every time the player restarts the game, a different background is chosen randomly to keep the experience fresh and interesting.

Audio:
🔹 Background Music: A lively and fun soundtrack that plays during the game.

User Interface:
🔹 Main Menu: Just click on the screen to start the game.
🔹 Game Screen: Shows the player's current score.
🔹 Game Over Screen: Displays the final score. Just click on the screen to start a new game.




Game Mechanics:
🔹 Rocks fall randomly from the top of the screen.
🔹 The speed of the rocks increases as the game goes on.
🔹 The player's score increases every time they successfully dodge a rock.
🔹 The game difficulty increases gradually to keep the player challenged and entertained.
