Welcome to the OVERDOSE Project

The "Overdose" project is a short film. Its goal is to raise awareness about the dangerous consequences of drug use and to inspire a healthy and positive life. Here is a description of the project:

Overdose
In a city neighborhood, four young people meet for a gathering that will change their lives forever. They only know each other because of drugs, and this meeting leads to a dramatic moment of reflection. A fifth boy, the drug dealer, arrives to sell them illegal substances for money. However, the story takes a tragic turn: after leaving the group, the dealer goes home and dies of an overdose in his bathtub.
The dealer's death becomes a turning point for the four teenagers. Feeling deep regret and realizing the devastating effects of drugs, they decide to change their lives completely. Each of them chooses a different path: one becomes a basketball player, another a kitesurfer, the third opens a gym, and the last one becomes a drummer.
Through their new passions and their commitment to a healthy life, these young people share a message of hope and positivity. The short film ends with a strong message against drug use, inviting viewers to think about their choices and to live a life free from drugs.
